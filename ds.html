DEAR SCARLETT

Dear Scarlett's branding required a subtly textured, yet sleek modern design. While they wanted to stick to a black and white color palette and a simple design, it was freshened up by the textures applied to the text and boxed outline.

Keeping to the black and white color scheme and straightforward design, the "Clarity" Shirt took some geometric inspiration and attempted to visualize their namesake song.

With the "Shame" Shirt, the black and white color palette was pushed slightly, while still retaining the modern quality of the other designs.
